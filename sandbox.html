what it is

a place to go from idea to something real.

Sandbox SC is an AI-native builder community at USC — open to any major, any skill level, no prerequisites.

It runs on a cohort model: students join each semester and ship projects through build nights, standups, mini-sprints, AI workshops, and demo days — backed by real tools (API credits, pro licenses) and mentorship from operators at top companies. AI has collapsed the distance between concept and product; Sandbox is built around that shift.

my role

media + events for a community that builds.

As an e-board member on media + events, I help shape how Sandbox shows up — its content and social presence, and the programming that brings student founders and builders together, from build nights to its flagship makeathon.

flagship event

the rêve makeathon.

A one-day creative sprint with Rêve (April 2026) — deliberately small, craft-first, and anti-pitch-deck. Students got full access to Rêve's AI creative tool and on-site mentorship, and kept full ownership of whatever they made that day.

It wasn't about chasing flashy innovation metrics. It was about shipping a finished, tangible thing in an afternoon — the whole Sandbox thesis, compressed into three hours.

"the distance between an idea and a made thing should be as small as possible."
